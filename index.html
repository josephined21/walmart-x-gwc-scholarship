Log In
If you have a Walmart account, you can either sign in with your information or open the Walmart app on your mobile device and scan the QR code on the touchscreen. If you have the Walmart app, you can create a shopping list before coming in-store, and the list will be synced to your cart's touchscreen. The touchscreen will display an optimized route navigating through the aisles so that you can pick up all the items you need in the shortest time possible. If you do not have a Walmart account, you may still use the touchscreen as a Guest. We strongly encourage you to create an account with Walmart, but we promise you will still have a great shopping experience without one!

Check Out
When you're ready to check out, tap the "Check Out" button on the touchscreen, and you can pay right away! If you are signed in to your Walmart account, your payment will process automatically. If you are not signed in, you will be prompted to go through a basic self checkout process. The touchscreen device is equipped with a chip reader and a card swiper for your convenience. After successful payment, an exit pass barcode will appear on the screen, and an employee will approve it at the exit while you are heading out. No need to wait in lines!

Sustainability

"As the world’s largest retailer, we have the ability to effect meaningful change for people and the planet. That’s why for more than a decade, we’ve made intentional efforts to reduce our waste and to increase recycling of plastic in our operations and by customers and suppliers."

What Walmart Is Doing About Plastic Waste

Recycled Plastic

The Walmart GoCart is made from 100% recycled plastic. The manufacturing does not involve chrome plating processes; chromic acid, hydrochloric acid, nickel chloride, nickel metal, nickel sulfate or sodium bisulphate are not used. The Walmart GoCart has a longer lifespan than conventional metal shopping carts because it does not rust.

Reusable or Paper Bags

Walmart is making an effort to reduce use of plastic bags in our stores. We strongly encourage shoppers to bring their own reusable shopping bags or purchase reusable bags during their shopping trips to Walmart. Paper bags are available upon request.
